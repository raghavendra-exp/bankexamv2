QUICK FORMULA REFERENCE

Bookmark These — They Cover 70% of the Quant Section

SIMPLE INTEREST
SI = (P × R × T) / 100
COMPOUND INTEREST
CI = P(1 + R/100)ᵀ − P
PROFIT %
= (Profit / CP) × 100
SPEED = DIST / TIME
km/h → m/s: × 5/18
WORK (COMBINED)
Time = AB / (A + B)
AVERAGE SPEED
= 2xy / (x + y)
CI − SI (2 YRS)
= P × (R/100)²
ALLIGATION RULE
Ratio = (x–m) : (m–y)

DO'S & DON'TS

Avoid These Mistakes — Learn from Others' Failures

✅ ALWAYS DO

  • Analyse every mock test error
  • Read newspaper daily (10 min)
  • Practice puzzles every day
  • Maintain an error log notebook
  • Use the 3-day revision rule
  • Sleep 7–8 hours every night
  • Start with strongest section first
  • Track your mock test scores
  • Practise with timer always on

❌ NEVER DO

  • Skip any single subject entirely
  • Guess randomly — -0.25 per wrong
  • Start new topics in final week
  • Compare scores with others
  • Cram the night before exam
  • Ignore GK/Current Affairs
  • Attempt mocks without analysis
  • Spend 3+ min on one question
  • Study 12 hrs in one day, then rest

CAREER GROWTH PATH

Where This Exam Takes You — Your Entire Future Journey

YEAR 0 · JOINING
🏦 Clerk / Junior Associate
Starting salary Rs. 26,000–31,000/month. Branch posting, induction training. Begin JAIIB preparation.
YEAR 2–4
📜 Clear JAIIB + CAIIB
+2 salary increments per exam. Mandatory for promotion. Highly recommended to clear within first 2–3 years.
YEAR 4–8
⭐ Special Assistant / Scale-I Officer
Internal promotion exam OR clear IBPS/SBI PO exam. Salary crosses Rs. 45,000–55,000/month at officer level.
YEAR 10–20
🏅 Manager → Senior Manager
Scale II and Scale III through performance + seniority. Salary Rs. 70,000–1,20,000/month + perks.
YEAR 25+
🏆 General Manager / Top Management
Possible to reach AGM / GM level through dedication. Full pension, medical, housing benefits for life.
