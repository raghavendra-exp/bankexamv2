Questions

Students ask us this

What exactly does this tool generate?
+
It generates structured study prompts — detailed instructions you paste into Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini, or any AI. The AI then acts as an expert tutor for that specific topic, exam, and year. One prompt gives you: concepts, solved examples, practice MCQs, PYQ patterns, shortcuts, and exam-day strategy.
Is this 100% free? Do I need to sign up?
+
Completely free — no account, no email, no payment. Open any tool, generate your prompt, copy and paste. Everything runs in your browser with zero data collection on your inputs.
Which AI gives the best results for Indian exams?
+
Claude (Anthropic) and ChatGPT-4o give the most detailed and accurate exam responses. Gemini is excellent for current affairs questions. Perplexity is best when you want web-sourced, real-time data. All 6 platforms are one-click accessible from inside any tool.
Are prompts updated for the current year's exam?
+
Yes — all tools auto-detect the current year using JavaScript and calibrate content for the latest exam cycle. PYQ ranges, cutoff data, and syllabus references all update automatically. No manual updates needed.
Can I use this on my phone?
+
Absolutely — all tools are fully mobile-responsive. Toggle sections, topic selectors, and one-click AI launchers all work perfectly on any screen size. Claude and ChatGPT both have mobile apps you can paste prompts into directly.
Do I still need to use textbooks or coaching?
+
These tools complement your preparation — they don't replace NCERT, standard reference books, or quality coaching. Use them to generate focused AI tutoring sessions on weak topics, get instant practice sets, and analyse PYQ patterns. Think of it as having a personal tutor available 24/7 at zero cost.
