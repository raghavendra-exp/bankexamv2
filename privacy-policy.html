ℹ️ About This Website

Indian Exam AI Prompt Generator (raghavbegins.github.io/bankexamv2) is a free educational platform operated by Raghavbegins, an individual content creator based in India. It provides AI-assisted study prompt tools for 80+ Indian competitive examinations — UPSC, GATE, CLAT, JEE, NEET, Banking, Railway, SSC, Defence, State PSC, Teaching, MBA/CAT, and Law entrance exams.

✅ No registration or login required. All AI prompt generation runs entirely in your browser — no prompt text or personal information is transmitted to our servers.
